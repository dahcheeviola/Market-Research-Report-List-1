<p><strong>The Dust Collection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Collection Systems</li><li>Electric Collection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dust collection systems are crucial for maintaining air quality in industrial environments, and they can be categorized into two main types: mechanical and electric collection systems. Mechanical collection systems utilize physical processes like filtration, cyclonic separation, or gravity to remove dust and particulates from the air. In contrast, electric collection systems employ electrostatic or electronic means to attract and capture dust particles. Each type has its own advantages, with mechanical systems often being simpler and more cost-effective, while electric systems can be more efficient for fine dust collection.</p></p>
