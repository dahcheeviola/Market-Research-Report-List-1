<p><p>The veterinary auscultator market predominantly features two types: single head and double head auscultators. Single head auscultators are designed for basic examinations, allowing veterinarians to listen to heart and lung sounds effectively in animals. In contrast, double head auscultators offer enhanced functionality, enabling simultaneous monitoring of multiple sounds, which is useful for more detailed assessments. Both types are essential tools in veterinary practice, aiding in diagnostics and improving animal care through accurate monitoring of physiological conditions.</p></p>
