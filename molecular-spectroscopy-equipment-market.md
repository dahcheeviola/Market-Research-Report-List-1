<p><p>The molecular spectroscopy equipment market encompasses various techniques used to analyze molecular properties. Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) spectroscopy allows detailed insights into molecular structures and dynamics. UV-Visible spectroscopy measures absorbance and transmittance in the ultraviolet to visible range, useful for identifying compounds and concentrations. Infrared (IR) spectroscopy focuses on molecular vibration, aiding in functional group identification. Near Infrared (NIR) spectroscopy, while similar to IR, targets overtones and combination bands, making it valuable for quantitative analysis and quality control in various industries.</p></p>
