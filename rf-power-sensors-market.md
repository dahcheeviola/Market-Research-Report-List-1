<p><strong>The RF Power Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat-based</li><li>Diode Detector-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF power sensors market primarily consists of two types: heat-based and diode detector-based sensors. Heat-based sensors measure power by detecting the heat generated from RF energy absorption, providing a broad dynamic range and high accuracy. In contrast, diode detector-based sensors utilize semiconductor diodes to convert RF power into a measurable voltage signal, offering faster response times and compact designs. Together, these technologies cater to various applications in telecommunications, aerospace, and industrial sectors, optimizing measurement and performance.</p></p>
