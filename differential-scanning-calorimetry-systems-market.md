<p><strong>The Differential Scanning Calorimetry Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Flux DSC</li><li>Power Compensated DSC</li><li>Modulated DSC</li><li>Hyper DSC</li><li>Pressure DSC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC) market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Heat Flux DSC** measures the heat flow difference between a sample and reference, ideal for thermodynamic studies.  </p><p>2. **Power Compensated DSC** uses a controlled heating element to maintain sample temperature, providing accurate thermal property measurements.  </p><p>3. **Modulated DSC** combines traditional and modulated heating rates to separate reversible and non-reversible thermal events.  </p><p>4. **Hyper DSC** enhances sensitivity for quick phase transitions.  </p><p>5. **Pressure DSC** allows analysis under varying pressure, useful for studying materials under specific conditions.</p></p>
