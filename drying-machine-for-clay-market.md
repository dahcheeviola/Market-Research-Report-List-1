<p><p>The drying machine market for clay features two primary types: the rotary dryer and the pressure spray drying machine. Rotary dryers utilize a rotating cylindrical drum to efficiently evaporate moisture from clay through direct contact with hot air. They are ideal for large-scale production. In contrast, pressure spray drying machines atomize slurry into fine droplets within a controlled environment, promoting rapid drying and preserving the clay's quality for porcelain applications. Both technologies cater to different production needs and clay types.</p></p>
