<p><p>A Floating Storage and Regasification Unit (FSRU) is a vessel designed to store liquefied natural gas (LNG) and convert it back into gas for distribution. The FSRU market comprises two main types: newly built units and converted vessels. Newly built FSRUs are purpose-designed for regasification, featuring advanced technology and efficiency. In contrast, converted FSRUs are traditional LNG carriers retrofitted for regasification purposes, often offering a cost-effective solution for operators looking to expand their import capacity without constructing new facilities.</p></p>
