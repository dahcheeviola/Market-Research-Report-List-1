<p><p>The high-performance servo drive system market primarily encompasses two main types: AC servo drives and DC servo drives. AC servo drives utilize alternating current to deliver precise control and efficiency, making them ideal for applications requiring high speed and torque. In contrast, DC servo drives operate on direct current, offering simpler control mechanisms and a quick response, suitable for lower speed and lower-power applications. Both types serve various industries, including robotics, automation, and CNC machinery, enhancing precision and performance.</p></p>
