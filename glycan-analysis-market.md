<p><p>The glycan analysis market encompasses several key technologies. Mass Spectrometry (MS) offers precise molecular characterization of glycans through mass-to-charge ratio detection. High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) separates glycans based on their size and polarity, providing detailed profiles. Capillary Electrophoresis (CE) utilizes an electric field to separate glycans by charge and size, enhancing resolution. Isoelectric Focusing (IEF) targets the separation of glycoproteins based on their isoelectric point, while Polyacrylamide Gel Electrophoresis (PAGE) separates biomolecules by size, aiding in glycan analysis.</p></p>
