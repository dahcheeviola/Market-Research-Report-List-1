<p><p>The automotive lighting components market includes several key types: Halogen lighting, HID (High-Intensity Discharge) lighting, and LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting. Halogen lights are traditional, offering good brightness and affordability despite lower energy efficiency. HID lights provide higher illumination and longer lifespan, making them popular in premium vehicles. LED lighting is gaining traction due to superior energy efficiency, durability, and design flexibility, making it ideal for modern automotive applications, including headlights, taillights, and interior lighting.</p></p>
