<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aerospace Gearbox Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AGB(Accessory Gearbox)</li><li>RGB(Reduction Gearbox)</li><li>Actuation Gearbox</li><li>Tail Rotor Gearbox</li><li>APU(Auxiliary Power Unit )Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace gearbox market consists of several key types, each serving specific functions in aircraft systems. The Accessory Gearbox (AGB) drives auxiliary components, while the Reduction Gearbox (RGB) transforms high-speed engine output to lower speeds for propeller or rotor use. Actuation Gearboxes control flight control surfaces, and Tail Rotor Gearboxes manage rotor orientation in helicopters. Finally, the Auxiliary Power Unit (APU) Gearbox supports ground power and engine start functions, making them integral to overall aircraft performance and efficiency.</p></p>
