<p><p>The chemical valves market comprises various types, each serving distinct functions. Gate valves are used for on/off control, providing minimal flow resistance. Control valves regulate flow and pressure through mechanical means, ensuring system stability. Ball valves offer quick shut-off capabilities with low flow resistance, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Butterfly valves permit efficient flow control with a simple design, suitable for large volumes. Other valves include check and solenoid valves, catering to specialized applications in chemical processes.</p></p>
