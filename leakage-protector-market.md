<p><p>The leakage protector market can be categorized into three main types: Voltage Type, Current Type, and Pulse Type. </p><p>Voltage Type leak detectors sense variations in voltage due to leakage currents, effectively preventing electrical shocks. Current Type devices monitor current imbalances between live and neutral wires, ensuring safety by disconnecting the circuit upon detecting leaks. Pulse Type systems use rapid electrical pulses to detect insulation failures, providing enhanced sensitivity and quicker response times. Together, these types offer diverse solutions for electrical safety across various applications.</p></p>
