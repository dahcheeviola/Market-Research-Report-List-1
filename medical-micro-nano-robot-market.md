<p><strong>The Medical Micro-Nano Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>'Self-Propelled Robot'</li><li>'External Field Propelled Robot'</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Micro-Nano Robot market is categorized into two main types: self-propelled robots and external field propelled robots. Self-propelled robots are designed to navigate autonomously within the human body, utilizing mechanisms such as flagella or micro-motors for movement. In contrast, external field propelled robots rely on external forces, such as magnetic or acoustic fields, to maneuver and deliver therapeutic agents or perform precise medical tasks. Each type offers unique advantages for targeted drug delivery and minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
