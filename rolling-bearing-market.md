<p><ul><li>Centripetal Type</li><li>Scroll Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rolling bearing market includes various types, primarily focusing on centripetal and scroll types. Centripetal bearings are designed to support radial loads and maintain stability in rotating machinery, ensuring efficient energy transfer. In contrast, scroll type bearings utilize a spiral-shaped mechanism to provide a smooth, low-friction operation, often found in applications like compressors and pumps. Both types enhance performance in diverse industries by reducing wear, improving reliability, and extending the lifespan of machinery.</p></p>
