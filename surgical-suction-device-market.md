<p><p>The surgical suction device market is divided into two main types: electric and pneumatic suction devices. Electric suction devices are powered by motors, providing efficient and consistent suction capabilities, making them suitable for various surgical procedures. They offer features like adjustable suction levels and portability. Pneumatic suction devices, on the other hand, rely on compressed air, offering a lightweight and cost-effective alternative. They are often used in settings where electricity is limited, delivering reliable performance in procedural environments.</p></p>
