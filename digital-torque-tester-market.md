<p><p>Digital torque testers are categorized into three main types based on their operation: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual torque testers require operator intervention for measurements, providing flexibility and control but potentially slower results. Semi-automatic testers streamline the process by assisting with measurement and data recording, enhancing efficiency while still involving some manual input. Automatic torque testers fully automate the testing process, offering rapid and precise measurements with minimal human involvement, making them ideal for high-volume production environments.</p></p>
