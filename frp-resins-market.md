<p><strong>The FRP Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Thermosetting Resins"</li><li>"Thermoplastic Resins"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FRP resins market is primarily divided into two types: thermosetting and thermoplastic resins. Thermosetting resins, such as epoxy and polyester, undergo a chemical change when heated, resulting in a hard, inflexible structure that's ideal for high-strength applications. In contrast, thermoplastic resins, like polypropylene and polycarbonate, can be melted and reshaped multiple times, offering versatility and recyclability. Each type caters to different industries, with thermosetting resins favored for durability and thermoplastics appreciated for ease of processing and lightweight properties.</p></p>
