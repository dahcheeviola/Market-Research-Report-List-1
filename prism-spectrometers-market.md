<p><p>The prism spectrometer market comprises three main types: light spectrometers, spectrographs, and photoelectric spectrometers. Light spectrometers analyze the spectrum of light to determine its composition, commonly used in various scientific and industrial applications. Spectrographs capture and record the spectral data, providing detailed analysis of light sources. Photoelectric spectrometers utilize photoelectric effects to measure light intensity and wavelength, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. Together, these instruments play crucial roles in material analysis, environmental monitoring, and research applications.</p></p>
