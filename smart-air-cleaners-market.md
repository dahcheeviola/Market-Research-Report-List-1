<p><ul><li>HEPA</li><li>Electrostatic Precipitators</li><li>Ionizers And Ozone Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart air cleaners utilize various technologies to enhance indoor air quality. HEPA filters capture airborne particles like dust and allergens, ensuring a high level of filtration. Electrostatic precipitators use charged plates to attract and trap particles, requiring minimal maintenance. Ionizers release negatively charged ions that bond with pollutants, making them heavier and easier to remove. Ozone generators produce ozone to neutralize odors and pollutants, though they may pose health risks at high concentrations. Each type addresses specific air quality needs and preferences.</p></p>
