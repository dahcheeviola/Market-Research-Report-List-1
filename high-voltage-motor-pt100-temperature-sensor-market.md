Bearing Type sensors are typically installed within motor bearings to monitor temperature, ensuring optimal lubrication and preventing overheating. Stator Embedded Type sensors are integrated directly into the stator windings, providing precise temperature readings of the motor's core components. Both types are essential for enhancing operational efficiency, extending motor life, and preventing thermal damage in high voltage motor applications.</p></p>
