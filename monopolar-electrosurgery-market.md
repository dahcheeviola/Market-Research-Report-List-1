<p><p>Monopolar electrosurgery is a technique that utilizes high-frequency electrical current to cut, coagulate, or desiccate tissue during surgical procedures. In this method, a single active electrode is used, which delivers the current to the target tissue, while the return electrode is placed on a different part of the body to complete the circuit. This approach allows for precise control of tissue manipulation, making it particularly useful for various surgical applications, including gynecological, urological, and general surgery.</p><p>The Monopolar Electrosurgery Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period. Additionally, continuous innovation and the introduction of new electrosurgical devices are enhancing efficiency and expanding their applications.
