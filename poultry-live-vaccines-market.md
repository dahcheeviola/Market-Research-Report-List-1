<p><p>The poultry live vaccines market comprises several key types targeting specific viral diseases. Newcastle Disease Virus (NDV) vaccines protect against a contagious viral infection affecting various poultry species. Marek's Disease vaccines prevent tumor formation and immunosuppression caused by the virus. Infectious Bursal Disease (IBD) and Infectious Bronchitis vaccines safeguard against immunosuppressive and respiratory diseases, respectively. Additionally, avian influenza vaccines are essential for controlling outbreaks of the highly pathogenic influenza virus in poultry, ensuring health and productivity within the sector.</p></p>
