<p><p>The back brace market comprises three main types: lower back braces, upper back braces, and lumbar braces. Upper back braces focus on stabilizing the thoracic region, correcting posture, and relieving discomfort. Lumbar braces specifically target the lumbar area, providing compression and support during physical activity or rehabilitation.
