<p><p>Antifouling paint and coating market consists of two primary types: hard paint and soft paint. Hard paints are durable formulations that create a robust surface to prevent marine organisms from attaching, often containing biocides for effective long-term protection. In contrast, soft paints are designed to release biocides gradually, ensuring ongoing effectiveness while also allowing for easier application and maintenance. Both types serve vital roles in maintaining the performance and lifespan of marine vessels, supporting efficient operation in aquatic environments.</p></p>
