<p><p>The modified firewood market is categorized into hardwood and softwood, each with distinct characteristics and uses. Hardwood, derived from deciduous trees, burns longer and hotter, making it ideal for heating and cooking. Softwood, from coniferous trees, ignites quickly and is often used for kindling or short-term fires. While hardwood tends to be more expensive due to its density and slower growth, softwood is more readily available and often less costly, catering to diverse customer preferences and needs.</p></p>
