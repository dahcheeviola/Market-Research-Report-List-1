<p><p>High Performance Air Source Heat Pumps (ASHP) are energy-efficient heating and cooling systems that utilize outdoor air to transfer heat. The market is primarily divided into two types: Air-to-Air and Air-to-Water. Air-to-Air ASHPs circulate air directly, heating or cooling indoor spaces effectively, making them ideal for residential and commercial applications. Air-to-Water ASHPs, on the other hand, produce hot water for heating systems, such as radiators or underfloor heating, and are suitable for both residential and larger industrial settings.</p></p>
