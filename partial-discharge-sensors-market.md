<p><strong>The Partial Discharge Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"UHF Sensor"</li><li>"HFCT Sensor"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Partial Discharge Sensors market comprises various types, notably UHF (Ultra High Frequency) sensors and HFCT (High Frequency Current Transformer) sensors. UHF sensors detect high-frequency electromagnetic waves generated by partial discharge activity in electrical insulation systems, offering non-invasive monitoring capabilities. On the other hand, HFCT sensors are designed to capture high-frequency currents associated with partial discharges in cables and equipment. Both sensor types are crucial for preventive maintenance and the early detection of potential failures in electrical systems, enhancing reliability and safety.</p></p>
