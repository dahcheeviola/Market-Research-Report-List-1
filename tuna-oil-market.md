<p><p>The tuna oil market comprises primarily two types: liquid tuna oil and micro-encapsulated tuna oil. Liquid tuna oil is a straightforward extraction of omega-3 fatty acids, commonly used in dietary supplements and food products due to its nutritional benefits. Micro-encapsulated tuna oil, on the other hand, involves encapsulating the oil in a protective coating, enhancing stability and minimizing oxidation. This form is favored in functional foods and supplements, as it improves digestion and maintains the efficacy of omega-3s over time.</p></p>
