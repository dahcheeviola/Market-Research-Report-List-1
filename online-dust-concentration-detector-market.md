<p><p>The Online Dust Concentration Detector Market features three main types of measurement technologies. Transmittance Measurement relies on light passing through a sample, with decreased intensity indicating higher dust concentration. Scattered Light Measurement involves illuminating a dust-laden air sample, where the scattered light quantifies particulate levels. Other methods may include multi-wavelength sensors and advanced imaging techniques. Each type serves to provide real-time monitoring of dust concentrations in industrial and environmental applications, ensuring compliance and health safety.</p></p>
