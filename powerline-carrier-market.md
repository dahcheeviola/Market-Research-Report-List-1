<p><p>Powerline Carrier (PLC) technology is categorized into two main market types: Narrowband PLC and Broadband PLC. </p><p>Narrowband PLC typically operates at lower frequencies, enabling long-range communication for applications like smart metering and home automation. It offers reliable data transmission over existing electrical infrastructure with lower bandwidth. </p><p>In contrast, Broadband PLC operates at higher frequencies, providing greater data rates suitable for internet access and multimedia applications. It supports higher bandwidths, enabling faster data transmission over power lines, making it ideal for residential and commercial use.</p></p>
