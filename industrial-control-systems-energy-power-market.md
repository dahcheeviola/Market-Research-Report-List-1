<p><p>Industrial Control Systems (ICS) in the energy and power sector include several key market types. SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) systems monitor and control decentralized assets. PLCs (Programmable Logic Controllers) are used for automation of electromechanical processes. DCS (Distributed Control Systems) manage complex, continuous processes across multiple locations. HMIs (Human-Machine Interfaces) facilitate operator interaction with machinery. Additionally, other systems encompass advanced process control, embedded systems, and cybersecurity solutions, enhancing operational efficiency and safety in energy and power management.</p></p>
